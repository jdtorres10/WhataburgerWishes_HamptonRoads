The Case for Hampton Roads
Hampton Roads is home to 1.79 million people, nine military installations, and 83,000+ active duty service members — with no Whataburger within 400 miles. The nearest location is in Charlotte, NC. Texas contributes more enlisted personnel to the US military than any other state, and Hampton Roads is the largest Naval concentration on the East Coast. That means a disproportionate share of those 83,000 service members grew up eating Whataburger. This project asks: where should the first location go?
